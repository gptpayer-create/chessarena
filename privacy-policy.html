Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

📋 This Privacy Policy explains how ChessArena collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website. Please read it carefully.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

ChessArena does not require account registration. We do not collect your name, email, or personal information unless you voluntarily submit it through our Contact form.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our site, our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) and third-party services may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL
  • Pages visited and time on page
  • Date and time of visit

1.3 Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and browser local storage for:

  • Preference storage: Saving your chosen language and dark/light theme.
  • Session cookies: Temporary, expire when you close your browser.
  • Analytics cookies: To understand how visitors use our site.
  • Advertising cookies: Placed by Google AdSense to serve relevant ads (see Section 3).

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site features (e.g. theme/language preferences will not be saved).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain ChessArena.
  • To remember your language and theme preferences (stored locally on your device).
  • To improve website performance and user experience.
  • To respond to contact form enquiries.
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense.
  • To detect and prevent technical issues.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Google AdSense and Advertising

ChessArena uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or www.aboutads.info.

For more information see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. GitHub Pages Hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages by GitHub, Inc. GitHub may collect technical visitor data as part of standard hosting operations. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.

5. Language and Theme Preferences

When you select a language or switch between dark and light mode, your preference is saved in your browser's localStorage. This data stays entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

ChessArena is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies.

8. Data Retention

We do not retain personal accounts or user profiles. Contact form submissions may be retained for up to 12 months. Language and theme preferences are stored only on your device and can be cleared by clearing your browser's localStorage.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to: access, correct, or delete your personal data; object to processing; and opt out of personalised advertising. To exercise any of these rights, contact us via our Contact Page.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
