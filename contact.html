Frequently Asked Questions

Is ChessArena completely free? ▼

Yes — 100% free. No subscriptions or paywalls. The site is supported by Google AdSense advertising.

Do I need an account to play? ▼

No account, no registration. Open the website and start playing immediately.

How do I change the language? ▼

Click the language button in the top-right corner of the header. Choose from 20 languages including Hindi, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Spanish, French, and more. Your selection is saved automatically.

How do I switch between dark and light mode? ▼

Click the ☀️/🌙 button in the header next to the language selector. Your theme choice is saved in your browser and will be remembered next time you visit.

How does fullscreen mode work? ▼

On the game page, click the "⛶ Fullscreen" button below the chess board. The board will expand to fill your entire screen. Click "✕ Exit" or press Escape to return to normal view.

How does the AI work? ▼

Our AI uses the Minimax algorithm with alpha-beta pruning — a classic technique that searches several moves ahead. It uses piece-value tables to make strategic decisions and provides a solid challenge for beginners and casual players.

Can I play on my phone? ▼

Yes! ChessArena is fully responsive and works on mobile phones, tablets, and desktops. Just open the website in your browser — no app download needed.

Are my game moves saved between sessions? ▼

Games are not saved when you close the browser tab — this is by design to protect your privacy. However, your language and theme preferences are saved and will be remembered next time.

I found a translation error. How do I report it? ▼

Use the contact form above and select "Language / Translation Issue" as your subject. Please mention which language, which part of the UI, and what the correct translation should be. We appreciate your help!
