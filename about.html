A Brief History of Chess

Chess originated in northern India during the Gupta Empire (6th century AD) as Chaturanga. It spread to Persia, then the Arab world, reaching Europe via Spain in the 10th century. Modern rules were fixed by the 15th–19th centuries. Today over 600 million people play chess worldwide, governed by FIDE (Fédération Internationale des Échecs).
